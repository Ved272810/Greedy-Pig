How to Play

Roll the dice and collect points! Everyone starts IN. Each roll adds the dice number to every player still in. If a 4 is rolled, the round ends immediately and all "in" players get 0! Players can click their name to go OUT. The game lasts 3, 5, or 10 rounds. Highest total wins!
